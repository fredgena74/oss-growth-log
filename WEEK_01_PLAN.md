# Week 1 Plan (OSS Growth)

## Targets (quality > quantity)
- 2 external PRs (docs/translation/examples)
- 1 issue/discussion contribution (triage, clarification, or reproduction steps)
- Keep a daily log (short, factual)

## Operating rules
- Prefer repos with active maintainers and clear CONTRIBUTING docs.
- Avoid trivial typo-only PRs unless they fix real ambiguity or broken links.
- Every PR must have: clear title + what/why/how.
